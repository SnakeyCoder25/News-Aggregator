Stock symbols are codes used to represent public companies. Each public company has there own unique stock symbol which can be found by searching online the company name and there stock symbol. These codes are normally 4 - 5 characters.


The stock search term is what key word you want to have in your news feed that is associated with the business. We know that searching for the busisness name isn't allwasy going to yeild the most relevant results for a particual business. The feature allows you to custermise your news feed. For the most part, we recomend putting in the names of the business's in your portfolio. Make sure to not type in things like inc. For example for APPL, try 'Apple' as the search term.


We only prove News from yesterday, to keep everything up-to-date. If there are no articles with the search term used, no results will come up. You might also not be using the right search term, ensure there are no mistakes, and try changing it.
